Sweet and Sour Chicken

Home

Description

The Sweet and Sour Chicken recipe is a twist on classic Sweet and Sour Pork that swaps out the protein for one that's leaner. It has the same great flavor and iconic sweet and sour sauce, perfect when you're craving the Traditional Chinese dish.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Carrot
  • Red Bell Peppper
  • Onion
  • Chicken Breast
  • Oil
  • Ketchup
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prep vegetables
  2. Prep chicken
  3. Prep batter
  4. Prep sauce
  5. Prep slurry
  6. Fry chicken and veggies
  7. Cook sauce & finish dish
